Welcome to my website! I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Developmental and Psychological Science at Stanford University, advised by Professors Ben Domingue and Jason Yeatman. My research interests focus on psychometrics and quantitative methods. Prior to Stanford, I have worked on Bayesian analysis, structural equation modeling, and network analysis with Professors Junhao Pan, Edward Ip, Xinya Liang, and Johnny Zhang.
